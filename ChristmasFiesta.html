Concept

La Noche de la Fiesta

The design anchors itself in the image of the Christmas star — a celestial light source illuminating a warm, intimate space below. The glowing greenhouse represents Dixon Gallery & Gardens as the physical home of the event: a space of cultivation, beauty, and community.

The typographic system divides roles precisely: Playfair Display Black in all-caps commands authority and presence; Great Vibes in gold introduces warmth and a distinctly Latin celebratory spirit; Montserrat SemiBold handles all event logistics cleanly. Deep black maximizes institutional partner logo contrast and lets the gold-and-white system breathe.
